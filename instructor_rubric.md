## A. Repository setup and professionalism (15)

## B. Content quality and completeness (35)
- (10) Home page: clear welcome, purpose, and navigation cues
- (10) About page: substantive bio (not placeholder text)
- (15) Project page: clear question/topic, data or motivation, methods/plan

## C. Site structure and usability (25)
- (10) Navbar present and functional across pages
- (10) Consistent headings, readable layout, and correct spelling/grammar
- (5) External links (if used) work and are appropriate

## D. Technical correctness (25)
- (15) Site renders without errors; required pages exist in `_site/`
- (5) No broken internal links (e.g., navbar targets exist)
- (5) GitHub Pages deploy succeeds (site is reachable)

## Notes for students
- You are graded on the rendered site AND the repository content.
- If a build fails, fix it and push again before the deadline.
